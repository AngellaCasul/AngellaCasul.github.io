The Thousandfold Treasure:

Gatekeeper's Challenge!

You're a treasure hunter searching for a legendary treasure hidden deep within a vast mountain range. A mystical gatekeeper controls access to the treasure chamber, and to proceed, you must solve a magical number puzzle.
The gatekeeper gives you a hint: "The number that unlocks the treasure is somewhere between 1 and 1000." However, you have 10 attempts to guess the number before the entrance to the chamber seals forever, leaving the treasure beyond your reach.
But be careful! The gatekeeper grows impatient with each incorrect guess, and if you use all your attempts, the treasure remains locked away for another 100 years.
